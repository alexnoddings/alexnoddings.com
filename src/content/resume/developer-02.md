### Summary

I joined this company (which traditionally offered managed services/low-code solutions) in early 2021 as a junior developer.
I leveraged my experience in mature development teams to shape the growth of their brand-new custom development team, and defined pivotal technological and development strategies.

In under 4 years, I worked my way up to Principal Software Engineer, reporting to the board.
My day-to-day was spent developing solutions, collaborating with stakeholders, and leading the team to deliver digital transformation and process improvements.

I also loved getting stuck into everything else the team did from time to time. On top of my usual project work, I:
- managed their DevOps processes
- defined solution standards/best practices
- designed their technology stack
- wrote internal systems and libraries
- ran interviews
- mentored colleagues
- whatever else was needed to keep the team running smoothly!

### Responsibilities

- Designed and lead the implementation of complex systems, made pragmatic decisions factoring in requirements, technical debt, and business priorities
- Consistently owned the delivery of large-scale new projects and changes, and collaborated with engineers and stakeholders to ensure successful outcomes
- Articulated difficult technical ideas with stakeholders internally and externally
- Proactively stayed up-to-date with technology trends, both in my area and across the board, and used that knowledge to inform our roadmaps
- Confidently tackled vague/complex requirements, and broke them down into actionable items and objectives
- Set multi-year tech strategies in line with technical requirements and business goals, getting broad buy-in, and bringing engineers on board
- Defined best practices and standards for non-functional project requirements (eg testing, performance, and security)
- Supported hiring efforts for new engineers, from junior through to senior
- Provided mentoring, constructive feedback, and assistance for engineers
- Acted as a reliable problem solver and escalation point for technical challenges
- Lead by example by creating high-quality designs, code, and documentation
- Supported existing and legacy systems to ensure clients faced minimal disruption
