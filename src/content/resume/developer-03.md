### Summary

My main role within this organisation is a Senior Full-Stack Developer, working in a multi-disciplinary squad to deliver and improve systems for both internal and public users.

On top of my regular work, I love working on difficult problems, such as getting stuck in with:
- Improving observability and traceability across applications to provide better insights into system health
- Proactively identifying and resolving pain points in the developer experience to improve developer velocity
- Advocating for the adoption of more modern technologies and promoting more efficient engineering processes

### Responsibilities

- Delivering high-quality, maintainable, well-tested code to ensure services are reliable and help improve organisational operations
- Collaborating with multiple other disciplines to efficiently transform business requirements into accessible, reliable software
- Reviewing code with constructive feedback, and mentoring and assisting other developers
- Contributing to defining technical strategies and non-functional requirements (eg testing, code quality, and maintainability) in line with business goals
- Championing sustainable, pragmatic technical debt management and reduction
- Scoping systems and applying appropriate design patterns and technologies to solve problems while minimising cost and time investment
- Supporting and improving legacy systems to ensure they continue to support changing operational requirements
